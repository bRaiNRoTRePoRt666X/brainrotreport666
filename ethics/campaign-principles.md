# Secretly Ethical Campaign Principles

## Overview

This campaign operates under a **secretly ethical** framework — we maintain
the highest ethical standards while creating compelling content that appears
effortless to the audience.

## Core Principles

### 1. Transparency (Hidden in Plain Sight)

**Public:** Entertaining, engaging content
**Private:** Full disclosure, honest messaging

- All sponsorships clearly disclosed
- No deceptive editing or misleading claims
- Sources documented and verifiable
- Corrections issued promptly

### 2. Consent & Privacy

**Public:** Seamless content experience
**Private:** Comprehensive consent management

- Written releases from all participants
- Clear usage rights documentation
- Privacy-respecting data collection
- Opt-in audience communication

### 3. Fair Compensation

**Public:** Professional production quality
**Private:** Fair pay for all contributors

- Documented payment rates
- Timely contractor payments
- No exploitation of volunteers
- Living wage commitment

### 4. Accuracy & Truth

**Public:** Confident, authoritative content
**Private:** Rigorous fact-checking

- Multi-source verification
- Expert review when needed
- Clear distinction: fact vs. opinion
- Retractions when wrong

### 5. Positive Impact

**Public:** Entertaining content
**Private:** Net-positive mission

- Community benefit consideration
- No harmful stereotypes
- Mental health awareness
- Environmental consciousness

### 6. Sustainability

**Public:** Consistent output
**Private:** Sustainable practices

- Reasonable production schedules
- Team wellness prioritized
- Resource efficiency
- Long-term thinking

## Ethics Checklist

Every episode must pass:

```
CREATIVE PHASE:
[ ] Script fact-checked
[ ] Claims verifiable
[ ] No harmful stereotypes
[ ] Disclosure requirements identified

PRODUCTION PHASE:
[ ] All participants signed releases
[ ] Location permissions obtained
[ ] Privacy considerations addressed
[ ] Safety protocols followed

POST-PRODUCTION PHASE:
[ ] No deceptive editing
[ ] Music/assets properly licensed
[ ] Disclosures added
[ ] Content warnings if needed

DISTRIBUTION PHASE:
[ ] Sponsor disclosures visible
[ ] Affiliate links marked
[ ] Age restrictions if needed
[ ] Community guidelines compliant

PUBLISH PHASE:
[ ] Final ethics review complete
[ ] Documentation archived
[ ] Feedback channels open
```

## Red Lines (Never Cross)

- ❌ No doxxing or revealing private information
- ❌ No harassment or targeted attacks
- ❌ No misinformation or disinformation
- ❌ No exploitation of vulnerable populations
- ❌ No undisclosed paid promotions
- ❌ No stolen or unlicensed content
- ❌ No manipulative dark patterns

## Ethics Review Process

1. **Self-Review:** Creator completes ethics checklist
2. **Legal Review:** Legal-compliance department verifies
3. **Final Check:** QC team confirms before publish
4. **Post-Publish:** Community monitors feedback

## Reporting Concerns

Any team member can flag ethical concerns:
- Anonymous reporting available
- No retaliation policy
- 24h response commitment
- Executive escalation path

## Annual Ethics Audit

- External ethics review
- Team feedback survey
- Policy updates
- Transparency report
