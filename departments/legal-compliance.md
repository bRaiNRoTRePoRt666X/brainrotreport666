# Legal & Compliance Department

## Role
Ensure all content is legally compliant and ethically sound.

## Responsibilities

### Contracts
- Talent agreements
- Contractor agreements
- Collaboration terms
- Release forms

### Rights Management
- Music licensing
- Stock footage licenses
- Image rights
- Usage permissions

### Privacy Compliance
- GDPR compliance
- CCPA compliance
- Data handling procedures
- Privacy policy adherence

### Ethics Oversight
- Content ethics review
- Disclosure requirements
- Conflict of interest checks
- Ethical guidelines enforcement

### Disclaimers
- Sponsor disclosures
- Affiliate disclosures
- Content warnings
- Legal disclaimers

## Deliverables

| Item | Type | Location |
|------|------|----------|
| Contracts | Legal docs | `departments/legal-compliance/contracts/` |
| Licenses | Rights docs | `departments/legal-compliance/rights/` |
| Privacy docs | Compliance | `departments/legal-compliance/privacy/` |
| Ethics guidelines | Policy | `departments/legal-compliance/ethics/` |
| Disclaimers | Templates | `departments/legal-compliance/disclaimers/` |

## Review Checkpoints

Every episode requires:
- [ ] Script ethics review (Creative handoff)
- [ ] Rights clearance (Production handoff)
- [ ] Final compliance check (Distribution handoff)
- [ ] Disclosure verification (before publish)

## Tools
- Contract management: DocuSign, PandaDoc
- Rights: Music libraries, stock sites
- Compliance: Internal checklists

## KPIs
- Compliance rate (100% target)
- Review turnaround (<24h)
- Zero legal issues
