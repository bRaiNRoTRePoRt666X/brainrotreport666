# Production Department

## Role
Capture all raw footage and audio for episodes.

## Responsibilities

### Raw Footage
- Main camera recording
- B-roll capture
- Screen recordings
- Footage organization

### Audio
- Voice recording
- Location audio
- Audio quality checks
- File naming conventions

### Assets
- Prop management
- Location coordination
- Talent coordination
- Equipment maintenance

### Logs
- Production logs per episode
- Timecode documentation
- Take notes
- Issue tracking

## Deliverables

| Item | Format | Location |
|------|--------|----------|
| Raw footage | MP4/MOV | `departments/production/raw-footage/` |
| Audio | WAV/MP3 | `departments/production/audio/` |
| Assets | Various | `departments/production/assets/` |
| Logs | Markdown | `departments/production/logs/` |

## Handoff Requirements

Before handing off to Post-Production:
- [ ] All footage captured
- [ ] Audio synced and cleaned
- [ ] Files organized and named
- [ ] Production log complete
- [ ] Best takes marked

## Tools
- Cameras: Sony, Canon, iPhone
- Audio: Zoom, Rode, Shure
- Software: OBS, DaVinci (capture)

## KPIs
- Footage quality score
- Audio quality score
- On-time delivery
- Reshoot rate (<10%)
