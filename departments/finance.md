# Finance Department

## Role
Manage budgets, track revenue, and ensure financial sustainability.

## Responsibilities

### Budget Management
- Episode budgets
- Department budgets
- Campaign budgets
- Budget tracking

### Invoicing
- Contractor payments
- Vendor invoices
- Payment scheduling
- Payment tracking

### Revenue Tracking
- Platform revenue (AdSense, etc.)
- Sponsorship income
- Merchandise sales
- Affiliate income

### Expense Tracking
- Equipment purchases
- Software subscriptions
- Contractor costs
- Operational expenses

## Deliverables

| Item | Frequency | Location |
|------|-----------|----------|
| Episode budget | Per episode | `departments/finance/budgets/` |
| Invoice tracker | Monthly | `departments/finance/invoices/` |
| Revenue report | Monthly | `departments/finance/revenue/` |
| Expense report | Monthly | `departments/finance/expenses/` |

## Approval Workflows

Required approvals:
- Budget >$500: Department head + Finance
- Budget >$2000: Executive approval
- New vendor: Finance + Legal

## Handoff Requirements

To departments:
- [ ] Budget allocation communicated
- [ ] Spending limits defined
- [ ] Expense reporting process shared

From departments:
- [ ] Expense reports submitted
- [ ] Invoice requests complete
- [ ] Budget variance explained

## Tools
- Accounting: QuickBooks, Xero
- Tracking: Spreadsheets, Notion
- Payments: PayPal, Wise, Stripe

## KPIs
- Budget adherence (>95%)
- Payment timeliness (100%)
- Profit margin tracking
- Cash flow health
