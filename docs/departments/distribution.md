# Distribution Department

## Role
Publish and optimize content across all platforms.

## Responsibilities

### YouTube
- Video uploads
- Title/description optimization
- Tags and categories
- End screens, cards
- Community posts

### Instagram
- Reels uploads
- Feed posts
- Stories
- Captions and hashtags

### TikTok
- Video uploads
- Caption optimization
- Hashtag strategy
- Trend integration

### Scheduling
- Release calendar management
- Optimal timing
- Cross-platform coordination
- Buffer management

### SEO
- Keyword research
- Title optimization
- Description writing
- Tag strategy

## Deliverables

| Item | Platform | Location |
|------|----------|----------|
| Published video | YouTube | `departments/distribution/youtube/` |
| Published reel | Instagram | `departments/distribution/instagram/` |
| Published video | TikTok | `departments/distribution/tiktok/` |
| Schedule | All | `departments/distribution/scheduling/` |
| SEO docs | All | `departments/distribution/seo/` |

## Handoff Requirements

After publishing:
- [ ] All platforms updated
- [ ] Links documented
- [ ] Analytics tracking enabled
- [ ] Community team notified
- [ ] Archive initiated

## Tools
- Scheduling: Buffer, Hootsuite
- SEO: TubeBuddy, VidIQ
- Analytics: Platform native tools

## KPIs
- On-time publishing rate
- Platform optimization score
- Initial 24h performance
- SEO ranking
